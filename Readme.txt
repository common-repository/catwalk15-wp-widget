The Catwalk15 Widget is a responsive widget that shows 6 latest images from a Catwalk15 user.

== Description ==

This advanced widget lets you beautifully showcase Catwalk15 photos on your blog or website.

* For your own photos
* Thumbnails with link to your posts
* Custom options: picture count, username, show achievements

== Installation ==

Go to the download page and grab the latest version. Extract everything in your Wordpress
plugins directory and enable it on your admin dashboard. You should now find a
new item in the Appearance menu called **Catwalk15 Widget**.

Play with the settings to get the one that suits best with your blog. Then go
the Widgets menu and place where you want it to appear.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I edit my photos count? =

Go to the widget options and change your photo count from the dropdown in the plugin.
